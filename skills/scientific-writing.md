# Scientific Writing

Assists with academic papers, technical reports, and structured analytical writing.

## Capabilities

### Paper Drafting
- Abstract, introduction, methodology, results, discussion, conclusion
- Citation placeholders in APA, MLA, IEEE, or Chicago format
- Passive/active voice calibration to match field conventions

### Literature Review
- Synthesizes multiple sources into a coherent narrative
- Identifies gaps, contradictions, and consensus positions
- Generates annotated bibliography entries

### Technical Reports
- Executive summary + detailed body structure
- Data presentation recommendations (tables vs figures)
- Recommendations section with supporting rationale

### Editing
- Clarity and concision improvements
- Jargon audit (simplify where appropriate, preserve where necessary)
- Logical flow and argument structure review

## Activation

> "scientific-writing: draft an abstract for a paper about LLM hallucination reduction techniques"
> "write a literature review section on transformer architecture evolution"
> "edit this methodology section for clarity"

## Tone

Default: formal academic English. Specify your target journal or conference for style calibration.
